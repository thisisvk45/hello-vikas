Lessons Learned

Key Takeaways

  • 01

    Keyword to Ad to Page alignment is everything. Keywords must appear in headlines or ad relevance suffers. Google rewards message consistency across the funnel.

  • 02

    Persona driven keywords win. Jill (lifestyle) outperformed Raj (professional) every time. Understanding how your audience actually searches matters more than who they are.

  • 03

    Budget discipline is critical. $498 unspent in Round 3 directly cost the backpack click goal. In paid search, unspent budget is a missed opportunity.

  • 04

    Display is not search. 454K impressions but only 31 conversions. Display drives awareness, but conversions need audience creative fit and intent signals.

  • 05

    Data compounds. Each round produced better data to optimize the next. By Round 4, every dollar was precision allocated based on historical performance.
